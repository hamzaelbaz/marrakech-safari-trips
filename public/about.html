Marrakech Safari Trips was created with a simple idea: to share the real beauty of Morocco with travelers from around the world. As a local team based in Marrakech, we have deep knowledge of the country, its culture, and its hidden gems.

Our mission is to provide authentic, comfortable, and well-organized journeys — from desert tours in the Sahara to private trips across Morocco. We focus on trust, quality service, and creating meaningful travel experiences that our guests remember long after their trip ends.

Everything You Need to Know Before Booking Your Morocco Tour

What types of Morocco tours do you offer?

We offer a wide range of Morocco tours including Sahara desert tours, day trips from Marrakech, coastal tours, and fully customized multi-day travel packages across Morocco.

Can I customize my Morocco itinerary?

Yes, all our tours can be customized based on your travel dates, interests, budget, and preferred destinations.

What is included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, private transport, professional driver/guide, and selected meals. Each tour page includes detailed inclusions.

How do you ensure the quality of your tours?

We carefully select experienced local guides, trusted partners, and reliable transport providers to ensure a safe, comfortable, and high-quality travel experience.

Do you offer eco-friendly or sustainable travel options?

Yes, we promote responsible tourism by working with local communities, supporting eco-friendly accommodations, and encouraging sustainable travel practices.
