Itinerary

Discover the best of Morocco on this unforgettable 6-day Imperial Cities, Atlantic Coast & Desert Tour from Rabat to Marrakech. This immersive journey takes you through Morocco’s most iconic destinations, from historic capitals and coastal gems to remote desert landscapes. Explore vibrant cities like Rabat and Casablanca, walk along the Atlantic coast in Essaouira and Agadir, and venture deep into the Sahara desert for a magical camel trek and night under the stars. Experience authentic Berber culture, dramatic mountain scenery, and breathtaking natural beauty all in one expertly crafted tour.

Day 1 – Rabat – Casablanca – El Jadida
  • Start your journey with a pick-up in Rabat and explore Morocco’s capital, including Hassan Tower, Mohammed V Mausoleum, Chellah Necropolis, and the Kasbah of the Oudayas overlooking the Atlantic Ocean.
  • Continue to Casablanca to visit the impressive Hassan II Mosque, the Habous Quarter, and the Art Deco city center.
  • Drive along the Atlantic coast to El Jadida for an overnight stay in this historic seaside town.

Day 2 – El Jadida – Safi – Essaouira
  • Travel along the scenic Atlantic coastline toward Essaouira with several panoramic stops along the way.
  • Enjoy a fresh seafood lunch upon arrival in Essaouira, the charming coastal city known as Mogador.
  • Explore the UNESCO-listed medina, the fishing port, and historic ramparts on a guided walk.
  • Overnight stay in Essaouira.
Day 3 – Essaouira – Agadir – Tafraoute
  • Depart Essaouira and head south along the Atlantic coast, passing through Agadir and beautiful seaside landscapes.
  • Witness the famous Argan trees with goats climbing them, a unique Moroccan sight.
  • Continue into the Anti-Atlas Mountains, discovering remote Berber villages and oases.
  • Arrive in Tafraoute and admire its famous pink granite rock formations glowing at sunset.
Day 4 – Tafraoute – Tata – Erg Chigaga Desert
  • Travel through the lush Ait Mansour Valley filled with palm groves and dramatic landscapes.
  • Continue toward Tata as the scenery transitions into arid desert terrain.
  • Arrive at the vast dunes of Erg Chigaga and enjoy a camel ride into the desert.
  • Spend the night in a traditional desert camp with dinner and stargazing.
Day 5 – Erg Chigaga – Mhamid – Zagora – Ouarzazate
  • Wake early to witness a breathtaking sunrise over the Sahara dunes.
  • Travel through M’hamid and visit the historic village of Tamegroute, known for its ancient library and underground kasbah.
  • Continue to Zagora and enjoy lunch before heading through the Draa Valley, filled with palm groves and traditional kasbahs.
  • Arrive in Ouarzazate for dinner and overnight stay.
Day 6 – Ouarzazate – Ait Ben Haddou – Atlas Mountains – Marrakech
  • Visit the famous UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Ben Haddou, one of Morocco’s most iconic ksars.
  • Cross the High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n’Tichka Pass with stunning panoramic views.
  • Enjoy scenic stops and lunch in the Atlas Mountains.
  • Arrive in Marrakech in the evening with drop-off at your accommodation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know Before Booking This Tour

Is this 6-day Morocco tour private?

Yes, this is a fully private tour from Rabat to Marrakech with your own vehicle, driver, and flexible itinerary.

Do we visit the Sahara Desert on this tour?

Yes, the tour includes a visit to Erg Chigaga desert with a camel trek and overnight stay in a traditional desert camp.

What type of accommodation is included?

Accommodation includes comfortable hotels and a desert camp with dinner and breakfast included.

Is the tour suitable for families?

Yes, this tour is suitable for families, couples, and small groups looking for a complete Morocco travel experience.

How long is the driving each day?

Driving times vary between 3 to 7 hours per day, with frequent stops for sightseeing, photos, and rest.

Can I customize this Morocco itinerary?

Yes, the itinerary can be fully customized based on your travel preferences, duration, and interests.
